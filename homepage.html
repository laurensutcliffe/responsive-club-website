Langho Running Club

Work out. Socialise. Have fun.

Anyone is welcome at our local running club. We work hard and achieve success. We even treat each other to a guilt-free social event too!

Langho Running Club is a freegroup for not only fitness finatics, but for those who are looking to meet new people too.
